About AudiVa

Protecting the digital world from the dangers of AI-generated voices.

Our Mission

AudiVa is created to address the rising challenges posed by AI-generated voices used in scams and misinformation. Our advanced algorithms help detect whether an audio is AI-generated or human, empowering individuals, businesses, and governments to safeguard their assets and maintain trust. With an accuracy of 95%, you can rely on AudiVa for precise predictions.
